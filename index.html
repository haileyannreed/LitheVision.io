LitheVision

Automated inspection built for real production environments. LitheVision brings research-backed computer vision into manufacturing and incoming quality control with minimal setup and integration effort.

Our Platform

LitheVision is a software platform for automated defect detection and quality inspection, designed to deploy easily across manufacturing and incoming quality control environments.

Flexible Inputs

Supports schematics, gold-standard reference images, and part or order metadata, allowing inspection to adapt to what customers already have available.

Robust Localization

Identifies and localizes defects under real-world variation in lighting, orientation, and image quality without tightly controlled imaging setups.

Deployment-First

Designed for low-cost cameras, minimal configuration, and seamless integration into existing inspection workflows.

Why LitheVision

Traditional automated inspection systems are expensive, rigid, and difficult to deploy. LitheVision was built to make automation practical for teams that have been priced out or left behind.

Lower Cost to Deploy

Operates without specialized hardware, precision lighting, or long integration cycles, reducing upfront and operational costs.

Real-World Ready

Works reliably despite variation in lighting, camera angle, and imaging conditions common in active production and warehouse environments.

Actionable Inspection

Provides defect classification, context, and repeat-pattern detection to support root-cause analysis and upstream quality improvement.
